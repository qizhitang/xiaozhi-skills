# 分年级口语话题库

> 本文档为 `xiaozhi-english-speaking-coach` 的配套参考资源，提供晨间热身、即兴演讲、苏格拉底四级跳的场景话题。

---

## 一、话题分级原则

```
话题选择三维度：
  ① 兴趣匹配度：优先使用学习DNA中记录的学生兴趣
  ② 词汇可及度：话题所需词汇 ≤ 学生当前词汇量的90%
  ③ 思维挑战度：每个话题按 "叙述→分析→假设→判断" 四级递进设计

话题难度标记：
  ⭐ 入门级（小学高年级/初学者）：只需叙述+简单原因
  ⭐⭐ 进阶级（初中/有一定口语基础）：可完成完整四级跳
  ⭐⭐⭐ 挑战级（高中/口语较流畅）：要求抽象推理与价值判断
```

## 二、小学高年级话题（⭐ 入门级）

### 日常生活类

| 话题 | 英文引导语 | 四跳适配提示 |
|-----|-----------|------------|
| 我的周末 | "What did you do last weekend? Tell me about it." | 第1跳用过去时叙述；第2跳问 "Why did you choose that?"；第3跳简化为 "What if it rained?"；第4跳跳过或简化为 "What's your favorite part?" |
| 今天的三餐 | "What did you eat today? Which meal was your favorite?" | 食物词汇丰富，适合练习描述性表达 |
| 我的好朋友 | "Tell me about your best friend. What makes them special?" | 适合练习人物描述（appearance + personality） |
| 放学后做什么 | "What do you usually do after school?" | 日常流程叙述，练习 first/then/finally |
| 我家的宠物（或想养的宠物） | "Do you have a pet? Or what pet would you like to have?" | 情感表达丰富，适合练习 "because" 句式 |

### 兴趣爱好类

| 话题 | 英文引导语 | 四跳适配提示 |
|-----|-----------|------------|
| 我最喜欢的游戏 | "What game do you like to play most? Why?" | 游戏规则叙述，适合练习顺序词 |
| 运动与比赛 | "Do you play any sports? Tell me about a game you played." | 动词丰富（run, kick, score, win） |
| 电视节目/动画 | "What's your favorite TV show? What happens in it?" | 叙述剧情，练习 third-person storytelling |
| 手工/画画 | "Do you like drawing or making things? Show me what you made." | 适合描述过程和成果 |
| 节日和庆典 | "What's your favorite holiday? How do you celebrate it?" | 文化词汇+情感表达结合 |

## 三、初中话题（⭐⭐ 进阶级）

### 校园生活类

| 话题 | 英文引导语 | 四级跳完整设计 |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| 课间十分钟 | "What usually happens during your 10-minute break?" | 1️⃣ What happens during break? → 2️⃣ Why do students behave this way? → 3️⃣ What if breaks were 30 minutes instead? → 4️⃣ What does a good break look like, and why does it matter? |
| 班级规则 | "What rules does your class have? Do you think they're fair?" | 1️⃣ Describe the rules → 2️⃣ Why were these rules made? → 3️⃣ What if there were no rules? → 4️⃣ What makes a rule "good" or "bad"? |
| 考试前的心情 | "How do you feel before a big exam?" | 情感词汇密集，适合练习 nervous/confident/worried/anxious |
| 社团活动 | "Are you in any school club? What do you do there?" | 适合描述+评价结合 |
| 和同学的冲突 | "Have you ever had a disagreement with a classmate? What happened?" | 四跳可深入到冲突解决的价值讨论 |

### 科技与未来类

| 话题 | 英文引导语 | 四级跳完整设计 |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| 手机该不该进校园 | "Should students be allowed to bring phones to school?" | 1️⃣ What's the current policy? → 2️⃣ Why do people disagree? → 3️⃣ What if phones were completely banned? → 4️⃣ What's the real issue—technology or self-control? |
| AI会取代老师吗 | "Will AI replace teachers one day? What do you think?" | 适合 technology/future 词汇 + 假设推理 |
| 社交媒体的利弊 | "What's good and bad about social media for teenagers?" | 四跳可深入到 "What does healthy online behavior look like?" |
| 太空旅行 | "If you could travel to space, would you? Why?" | 想象力+科学词汇结合 |
| 网课vs线下课 | "Which do you prefer—online classes or face-to-face classes?" | 适合比较句式（compared to / rather than / whereas） |

### 社会与思考类

| 话题 | 英文引导语 | 四级跳完整设计 |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| 什么是"公平" | "What does 'fair' mean to you? Can you give an example?" | 1️⃣ Describe a situation → 2️⃣ Why was it fair/unfair? → 3️⃣ What if the rules changed? → 4️⃣ What does fairness really mean? |
| 做志愿者的经历 | "Have you ever volunteered? What did you do?" | 适合叙述+价值讨论（It matters because...） |
| 环保行动 | "What can teenagers do to help the environment?" | 问题→原因→假设→行动逻辑完整 |
| 榜样与偶像 | "Who do you admire? What makes them worth admiring?" | 适合人物评价+价值判断 |
| 压力与放松 | "How do you deal with stress? What helps you relax?" | 情感表达+方法描述+效果评价 |

## 四、高中话题（⭐⭐⭐ 挑战级）

### 学术思辨类

| 话题 | 英文引导语 | 四级跳重点 |
|-----|-----------|----------|
| 成功的定义 | "What does success mean? Is it the same for everyone?" | 第4跳重点：So what—Does society's definition limit individual potential? |
| 教育的目的 | "What's the real purpose of education? Is it just to get a job?" | 适合 therefore/consequently 等高级逻辑词 |
| 科技伦理 | "Should there be limits on what technology can do?" | 假设+价值判断密集，需用 conditional + moral reasoning |
| 全球化与本土文化 | "Is globalization destroying local cultures?" | 第3跳重点：What if every country became the same? |
| 信息过载 | "We have more information than ever. Is that always good?" | 适合 paradox/irony 表达 |

### 职场预演类

| 话题 | 英文引导语 | 四级跳重点 |
|-----|-----------|----------|
| 团队合作vs独立工作 | "Do you prefer working alone or in a team?" | 适合比较+条件句（If I work alone, I can... whereas in a team...） |
| 领导力 | "What makes someone a good leader?" | 适合抽象评价+举例论证 |
| 时间管理 | "How do you manage your time when you have many tasks?" | 适合叙述方法+评价效果+提出改进 |
| 跨文化交流 | "Have you talked with someone from another culture? What did you learn?" | 适合 cultural awareness 表达 |
| 面试模拟 | "Imagine you're interviewing for a position. How would you present yourself?" | 实用场景+自我评价+压力表达 |

## 五、苏格拉底四级跳适配话题精选

以下话题经过专门设计，确保四跳每一跳都有充分的思考空间：

### 两跳版（小学/初学者适用）

```
话题：我最喜欢的食物
第1跳 What happened? → "What's your favorite food? Describe how it looks, smells, and tastes."
第2跳 Why? → "Why do you like it more than other foods? Is it the taste, or is it connected to a memory?"
（第3-4跳暂不启用，避免认知负荷过高）
```

### 三跳版（小学进阶/初中初期适用）

```
话题：校园运动会
第1跳 What happened? → "Tell me about the most exciting moment at your school sports day."
第2跳 Why? → "Why was that moment exciting? What made it different from other moments?"
第3跳 What if? → "What if your school didn't have a sports day? Would students miss something important?"
（第4跳视情况启用）
```

### 四跳完整版（初中以上适用）

```
话题：手机与学习
第1跳 What happened? → "Describe how phones are used in your school—by students and teachers."
第2跳 Why? → "Why do some schools ban phones while others allow them? What's the reasoning behind each approach?"
第3跳 What if? → "What if every student had a phone but could only use it for learning—would that change the debate?"
第4跳 So what? → "So what does this tell us about self-discipline and technology? Is the real problem the phone, or the person using it?"
```

## 六、话题选择决策流程

```
晨间热身 Step 3 话题选择流程：

① 检查学习DNA → 有记录的兴趣话题？
   → YES：使用档案话题（学生最熟悉的领域）
   → NO：进入②

② 检查口语DNA → 有上次未完成的话题？
   → YES：延续上次话题（连贯性优先）
   → NO：进入③

③ 使用本话题库 → 按学生年级选择难度区间
   → 随机选择一个话题类别
   → 如果学生在3分钟内聊不完一个话题 → 降级到更简单的类别
   → 如果学生聊得流畅 → 下次升级难度
```

## 七、话题扩展规则

```
当学生说 "这个话题我没兴趣" 时：
  → 不换话题，而是换角度：
    "你对 [原话题] 没兴趣没关系——
     那你觉得 [话题里哪个部分] 有意思？
     我们聊聊你关心的那个部分。"

当话题聊完但时间还没到时：
  → 从同一话题进入苏格拉底追问：
    "刚才你说 [学生观点]——
     我好奇的是，为什么会这样？"

话题库可持续扩展的来源：
  ① 学生在对话中自发提到的新话题 → 记入兴趣档案
  ② 当前热门新闻/事件（学生能接触到的）→ 随时补充
  ③ 教材单元主题 → 与课内学习联动
```
