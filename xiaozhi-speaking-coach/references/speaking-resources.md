## 一、分年级口语话题库

> 按年级分层，结合苏格拉底四级跳难度适配。
> 选题原则：学生有真实感受，能引发真实表达，不需要专业知识。

### 小学高年级（10-12岁）
适合第一跳和第二跳为主：
- My favorite animal and why
- What I did last weekend
- My best friend and what we do together
- My favorite food and how to make it
- A movie or cartoon I watched recently

### 初中（13-15岁）
适合完整四跳，话题有一定深度：
- Social media: good or bad for students?
- Should students have smartphones at school?
- My dream job and why
- A book/movie that changed my thinking
- What makes a good friend?
- Is it better to study alone or with others?
- How has technology changed our lives?

### 四级跳难度参考配置

**基础版（第1周）**
话题：My favorite season / What I ate for breakfast
完成目标：第一跳（叙述）流利即可

**进阶版（第3-4周）**
话题：Should schools have longer summer breaks?
完成目标：全部四跳，第四跳有完整观点

## 二、5套真实场景角色扮演完整脚本

### 场景①：机场值机+安检

**小龙虾扮演：** 机场工作人员（Check-in staff）

**学生任务：** 完成值机和安检，用英语应对以下情境：

关键对话节点（小龙虾按顺序推进）：
1. "Good afternoon! May I see your passport and ticket please?"
2. "Would you like a window seat or an aisle seat?"
3. "Do you have any checked baggage today?"
4. "Your boarding gate is B12, boarding starts at 2:30. Enjoy your flight!"

**安检过渡（小龙虾扮演安检员）：**
5. "Please place all electronic devices in the tray."
6. "Do you have any liquids in your bag?"

**学生目标词汇：** passport, boarding pass, aisle, carry-on, check in, security check

---

### 场景②：商店购物比较商品

**小龙虾扮演：** 运动鞋店导购

**学生任务：** 比较两款鞋，做出购买决定

关键对话节点：
1. "Welcome! Can I help you find something today?"
2. "We have two popular models right now—the Air Run and the Speed Pro. [介绍特点]"
3. "What do you mainly use sneakers for—running, basketball, or everyday wear?"
4. "The Air Run is on sale this week—20% off. Are you interested?"

**学生目标词汇：** comfortable, lightweight, durable, fit, discount, try on

---

### 场景③：西餐厅点餐结账

**小龙虾扮演：** 西餐厅服务员

**学生任务：** 点餐、询问、结账

关键对话节点：
1. "Good evening! Here's the menu. Can I start you off with something to drink?"
2. "Today's special is grilled salmon with vegetables. It's very popular."
3. "How would you like your steak—rare, medium, or well-done?"
4. "How was everything this evening? Are you paying by cash or card?"

**学生目标词汇：** starter, main course, dessert, well-done, bill, recommend

---

### 场景④：2分钟即兴演讲+提问

**小龙虾扮演：** 演讲听众+提问者

**话题示例：**
- "Talk about a person who has influenced you the most."
- "Describe a place you would love to visit and why."
- "What is the most important skill for young people today?"

**演讲后，小龙虾提2个追问：**
1. 内容深化类："You mentioned [学生提到的点]—could you give a specific example?"
2. 观点挑战类："Some people might disagree with you because [对立观点]. How would you respond?"

---

### 场景⑤：学校社团面试

**小龙虾扮演：** 英语社团面试官

**学生任务：** 完成英语社团社长申请面试

面试问题序列：
1. "Please introduce yourself briefly in English."
2. "Why do you want to be the president of our English club?"
3. "What activities would you organize for the club if you were elected?"
4. "Can you describe a time when you showed leadership?"
5. "Do you have any questions for us?"

**评估后反馈格式：**
"Based on this interview, here are my observations:
 Strength: [具体亮点]
 Area to work on: [1个具体建议]"

## 三、中国学生高频发音弱点与纠正方法

### 最高频10个发音问题

| 发音问题 | 中国学生常见错误 | 正确发音要点 | 练习句 |
|---------|--------------|------------|-------|
| th (θ) | 发成 s 或 t | 舌尖轻抵上齿，吐气 | "Think of three things." |
| th (ð) | 发成 z 或 d | 舌尖轻抵上齿，振动 | "This is the other one." |
| r (英) | 发成中文"r"卷舌 | 舌头不碰上颚，收拢 | "Run around the river." |
| v | 发成 w 或 b | 上齿咬下唇，振动 | "Very vivid video." |
| 单词尾音 | 把尾音全部吞掉 | 尾音要收，但不重读 | "I want to eat it." |
| 重音位置 | 平均分配重音 | 按单词重音模式处理 | "photograph / photography" |
| 长元音 | 发成短音 | 拉长发音时长 | "He leaves in the evening." |
| 辅音连缀 | 在辅音间加元音 | "street" 不要发成 "si-tree-te" | "Strong spring streams." |
| 语调（陈述句） | 句尾上扬（中式） | 陈述句句尾降调 | 对比练习 |
| 连读 | 每个词分开发 | 词尾辅音+词首元音连读 | "an apple / turn it off" |

### 纠音训练三步脚本

```
第一步：定位
"你刚才说 '[单词]'——我听到的是 [描述学生发音]。
 正确的发音是：[示范]。
 区别在于：[一句话说明发音要点]"

第二步：模仿
"现在跟我来：
 先单独发这个音：[音]
 然后加上这个词：[单词]
 然后放进句子里：[练习句]"

第三步：验证
"再来一遍，这次我来听：[学生跟读]
 [如果对了] 好多了！记住这个感觉。
 [如果还有问题] 还差一点——[具体指出哪里]，再试一次。"
```
